Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

Discovering Nairobi - A website designed to guide residents and visitors through Nairobi’s unique attractions, hidden gems, and cultural experiences.

2. Site Purpose

This website provides a local guide to Nairobi, offering:

  • Information on major attractions like Nairobi National Park and Karura Forest.
  • Best places to eat, including local street food and fine dining options.
  • Hidden gems that many locals and tourists are unaware of.
  • Travel tips covering safety, transportation, and budget travel.
  • A Contact Us page for inquiries and suggestions.

3. Scenarios

  • What are some must-visit places in Nairobi beyond the usual tourist spots?
  • Where can I find the best local food and cultural experiences?
  • How do I navigate Nairobi affordably and safely?

4. Color Schema

Primary Color: Dark Green (#005A32) – Navigation and headings.

Secondary Color: Warm Yellow (#FFC107) – Buttons and accents.

Background Color: Off-White (#F8F9FA) – Page background.

Text Color: Dark Gray (#333333) – Readable content.

5. Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat (Sans-serif) – Used for section titles.

Body Text Font: Open Sans (Sans-serif) – Used for paragraphs.

Accent Font: Lobster (Cursive) – Used for branding or highlights.

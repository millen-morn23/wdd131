Explore Nairobi's Key Attractions

Discover the must-visit spots that make Nairobi one of Africa’s most exciting cities.

Nairobi National Park

See lions, rhinos, and giraffes just minutes from the city center. It’s a rare wildlife experience in a capital city.

Giraffe Centre

Feed and interact with endangered Rothschild giraffes and learn about conservation efforts in Kenya.

Karura Forest

A peaceful escape within the city featuring waterfalls, caves, cycling trails, and rich biodiversity.
